We launched Trello on September 13th, 2011. We hoped to bring a new way to collaborate to a broad audience, and we wanted it to be free. Energized by your early support, we've been working hard ever since to make Trello more useful, faster, and available on all of your devices.

It’s now sixteen months later, and we’ve just had our 1,000,000th sign up. That’s a lot of users, a lot of validation, and a lot of trust placed in us. At this milestone, we’re peeling our eyes off our screens (of all sizes) for a moment to say this: Thank you!

Thanks for using Trello. Thanks for telling us what's working and what could be better. Thanks for inviting your co-workers, friends, and family to use Trello with you.

Sincerely,

The Trello Team
